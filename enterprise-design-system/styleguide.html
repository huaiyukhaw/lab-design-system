The visual language of PromptLab, rendered.

Cream canvas, near-black ink, a clay accent, and an editorial mono-eyebrow rhythm. Every value below is pulled live from tokens.css in this folder — this page is the visual companion to the spec in foundations.md and components.md.

01 · Foundations

Color

Toned whites and near-blacks. Cool throughout — neutrals use a cool slate-gray family. Clay is the single (blue) accent; the action surface is ink. Moss and sky are supporting brand hues.

02 · Foundations

Typography

Bricolage Grotesque for headings & labels, Hanken Grotesk for body, JetBrains Mono for eyebrows, code, and numeric data. Role-driven sizes, not a deep ladder.

Display — Bricolage Grotesque

Body — Hanken Grotesk
